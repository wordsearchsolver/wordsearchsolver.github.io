How to Use the Crossword Solver

Three ways to find your crossword answer — from simple pattern matching to advanced filtered search.

1

Enter Your Pattern

Type your pattern using capital letters for known positions and ? for unknown letters. For a 5-letter word starting with S: S????

2

Add Constraints

Optionally add letters the word must contain or must not contain. This narrows results when you have crossing letter clues from other answers.

3

Find Answers

Click "Find Answers" to search the dictionary. Results are highlighted to show which letters match your known positions.

4

Refine & Solve

Sort by length or alphabetically. Click any result to copy it. Use recent searches to quickly revisit previous patterns as you work through the puzzle.

How Crossword Pattern Matching Works

Our crossword solver uses regex-style pattern matching against a comprehensive English word dictionary. When you enter a pattern like C??SS, the solver constructs a regular expression that matches any 5-letter word starting with C and ending in SS — returning results like CROSS, CLASS, CHESS, and CRASS in milliseconds.


The "Must Contain" and "Must Not Contain" filters add additional constraints beyond the positional pattern. This is especially powerful when you've already solved crossing answers in the grid and know which letters must appear in the unknown word.

Crossword Solver vs Crossword Cheat

There's a meaningful difference between solving a crossword and cheating at one. Our tool is designed as a learning aid and accessibility tool — not just an answer machine. Results show all possible words matching your pattern, requiring you to use context clues, crossing letters, and puzzle theme knowledge to choose the right answer.


For crossword constructors and puzzle editors, the solver doubles as a fill finder — quickly identifying which words fit a given slot length and pattern during grid construction. This is the same technique used by professional crossword constructors.

FAQ

Crossword Solver — Frequently Asked Questions

What does the ? wildcard mean in the crossword solver?+

A question mark (?) represents any single unknown letter. If you have a 6-letter clue and know the first letter is F and last is T, enter F????T. Use one ? for each blank square in the crossword grid. You can also use a dot (.) as an alternative wildcard character.

How do I find a word when I only know some crossing letters?+

Use wildcards for unknown positions and enter known letters in their exact positions. If you know the word is 7 letters, the 3rd letter is E, and the 6th letter is T, enter ??E??T?. Then add any crossing letters you know to the "Must Contain" field to narrow results further.

How large is the crossword solver dictionary?+

The solver uses a built-in word list, and if you place a words.txt file in the same directory, it automatically loads the full 450,000+ word database. The larger database includes rare words, variant spellings, and obscure vocabulary often found in challenging crossword puzzles like the NYT or Times cryptic.

Can I use the crossword solver for cryptic crosswords?+

Yes, with some adaptation. Cryptic crossword answers often have unusual or archaic words — the full 450K dictionary helps here. The pattern matcher works the same way: if you've worked out from the wordplay that the answer is a 7-letter word with certain letters, enter those positions and the solver will find candidates.

Does the solver handle proper nouns and names?+

The built-in word list focuses on common vocabulary. With the full 450K words.txt file, coverage of proper nouns, place names, and personal names improves significantly. For specialized crosswords (geography, celebrity names, etc.), you may still need to verify answers against external sources.
