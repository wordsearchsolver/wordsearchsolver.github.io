FAQ

Printable Word Search — Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save the puzzle as a PDF?+

Click "Print Puzzle" or "Print + Key" to open your browser's print dialog. In the dialog, change the destination to "Save as PDF" (Chrome/Edge) or select "PDF" from the printer dropdown (Safari/Firefox). The resulting file will be a clean, paper-optimized PDF.

Will the answer key print on a separate page?+

Yes. When you click "Print + Key", the print output includes two pages: page 1 is the clean puzzle for students, and page 2 is an identical grid with all hidden words highlighted in yellow — the teacher's answer key. These print as separate sheets.

Can I customize the puzzle title and instructions?+

Absolutely. You can set a custom puzzle title (e.g., "Chapter 5 Vocabulary") and a subtitle/instruction line (e.g., "Find all the science terms below"). These appear prominently on the printed puzzle sheet.

What paper sizes does the print tool support?+

The tool supports both A4 (international standard) and US Letter (8.5×11 inch) paper sizes. Select your paper size in the settings before generating. The grid layout scales appropriately for each format.

Is this free for commercial use in activity books?+

The puzzles you generate are yours to use freely — including in commercial activity books, worksheets for sale, or educational materials. We only ask that you don't resell access to the tool itself.
