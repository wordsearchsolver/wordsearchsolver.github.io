Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Your privacy is not an afterthought — it's a fundamental part of how Word Search Solver is built.

📌 In a nutshell: We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal data. The Word Search Solver runs entirely in your browser. No servers, no tracking, no hidden surprises.

1. No Data Collection — Ever

Word Search Solver is designed to be a completely private tool. We do not collect, process, or store any information about you, your device, or your usage patterns. There are no analytics scripts, no tracking pixels, no cookies for tracking purposes, and no third-party data collection services embedded in this website.

Everything you type into the grid input or words field stays inside your web browser. When you click "Solve," the word search algorithm runs locally using JavaScript on your own device. No data is ever transmitted to a server — not even temporarily.

2. What Information Is Never Collected

Because we don't have accounts, logins, or backend storage, the following types of information never touch our systems:

  • Personal identifiers: Name, email address, IP address, or any contact details.
  • Puzzle data: The letters you paste into the grid, the words you search for, or any results generated.
  • Usage analytics: How often you use the tool, which features you click, or how long you stay on the page.
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, or hardware identifiers.
  • Location data: GPS coordinates, Wi-Fi triangulation, or IP-based geolocation.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

Word Search Solver does not use cookies for tracking or analytics. However, like all websites, your browser may store temporary files (cache) to load the page faster on repeat visits. These are managed entirely by your browser settings and contain no personal information.

We do not use localStorage or sessionStorage to remember your puzzles or any user data. Once you close or refresh the page, all inputs disappear completely.

4. Third-Party Services

Word Search Solver is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard server logs (such as request timestamps and the page requested) as part of their infrastructure. These logs are governed by GitHub's Privacy Policy and are not accessible or controlled by us.

We use no external APIs, advertising networks, or analytics providers. The fonts used are loaded from Google Fonts; however, these requests are standard web functionality and do not involve any tracking specific to your puzzle-solving activity. You can review Google's Privacy Policy for font-related data practices.

5. Children's Privacy

Word Search Solver is suitable for all ages, including children under 13. Because we collect no personal information from any user, there is no risk of inadvertently gathering data from minors. Parents and teachers can confidently use this tool in educational settings without privacy concerns.

6. Data Security

Since we never receive or store your data, there is no risk of data breach, loss, or unauthorized access from our side. All processing occurs locally in your browser's memory and is discarded as soon as you navigate away or close the tab. For your own security, we recommend using an up-to-date browser and keeping your device free from malware — standard best practices for any online activity.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we ever make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Any changes will be minor and will never introduce data collection without clear notice. We encourage you to review this page occasionally to stay informed.

8. Contact Information

Word Search Solver is an open-source educational tool. If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our practices, you can open an issue on our GitHub repository (linked in the footer) or contact us via the repository's discussion board. We are committed to transparency and will respond to legitimate inquiries as quickly as possible.

By using Word Search Solver, you acknowledge that no data is collected from you and that the tool operates entirely client-side. You are not required to agree to any terms beyond the standard operation of a static website.
